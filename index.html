FAQ

Answers, fast

Everything you need to know to get started confidently.

How long to launch a campaign?
Most campaigns launch in 3–7 days. Day 1–2: brief + setup. Day 2–4: AI matches + creator applications. Day 4–6: shortlist, negotiate, contract. Day 7+: content production. Express 24–48h available.
How are influencers verified?
API checks for follower/ER validation, anomaly detection for bots, manual review for high-value deals, historical performance tracking, and audience geo-language consistency tests.
Is BrandLink good for startups?
Yes—our free Starter plan supports 10 connections and 1 campaign. Micro-creators (10–50K) often drive the best cost-per-action for early-stage brands.
Are payments secure?
Escrow with PCI-DSS gateways. Funds release on deliverable approval. UPI, cards, net banking, and intl support. All data encrypted at rest and in transit.
Do you support multiple platforms?
Instagram, YouTube, X, LinkedIn, Snapchat, and more. Compare channel performance and consolidate reporting in one dashboard.
How does the AI matching work?
We model creator-audience fit using 50+ signals: demographics, interests, ER normalized by niche, content style vectors, brand affinity, authenticity, language, geo, and post-funnel conversions.
What if content isn’t approved?
Built-in approvals with 2–3 revision rounds. Mediation support for disputes. Escrow protects both parties until final approval.
Do you offer APIs and integrations?
Yes—webhooks and REST APIs for CRM, Slack, GA4/Looker, UTM link generators, and BI exports. Enterprise SSO and SCIM on request.
